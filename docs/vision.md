# Vision — The Network

> Version: 0.1 | Date: 2026-06-05

---

## One-Sentence Purpose

The Network is a flexible platform for empowering communities to safely
organize, share, and decide together.

---

## What Success Looks Like (3–5 Years)

- Communities of all kinds — families, clubs, vulnerable groups, and towns —
  adopt it and adapt it to their own needs instead of forcing their needs
  into a rigid tool.
- At least one town uses it as part of its government process, letting
  residents propose, discuss, and vote on local issues from their phones
  or computers.
- New "flavors" of the platform appear (special-needs family hub,
  community bulletin board, vulnerable citizens' hub), all built on the
  same core philosophy of privacy, data ownership, and shared decision-making.

---

## Core Promise

- A **safe place to coordinate and decide** where people's data and
  vulnerabilities are respected.
- **Flexible by design** — the community, not the software, decides what
  features, rules, and structures it needs.
- Over time, a **common civic layer** that towns, families, and groups can
  plug into to make participation easier and more humane.

---

## Defined Use Cases

### Special-Needs Family Community
A web-focused space that tailors information and local resources to each
family's profile, connecting them with others in similar situations without
overexposing personal details.

### Community Bulletin Board (Craigslist-like)
A listing and discovery hub for services, items, and opportunities that
bakes in The Network's trust, reputation, and privacy guarantees instead
of relying on raw, anonymous classifieds.

### Vulnerable Citizens Community
A protected environment where people at risk (due to health, status,
identity, or circumstance) can express needs, feedback, and votes in ways
that are heard by institutions but do not compromise their safety.

### Town Governance Layer
A civic participation layer where residents propose, deliberate, and vote
on local issues — results anchored publicly, identities protected.
